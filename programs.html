FTC Team 27406 – Impulse

Founded: 2024

Born from a desire to broaden student access to FTC, Impulse has truly hit its stride. Now in their second season, the team has paired a relentless competitive edge with a steadfast commitment to Gracious Professionalism. Proving themselves as both respected peers and formidable opponents on the field.

  • 2025
  • LAFTC Slidell Qualifier - Finalist Alliance Captain

  • New Orleans Qualifier
  • Louisiana Championship – Dean's List Award (Zakk Jackson)

FLL Team 65003 – Ignition

Founded: 2024

Ignition introduces younger students to robotics through the FIRST LEGO League Challenge. They competed in the 2024–2025 SUBMERGED season, advancing to the Louisiana State Championship and gaining valuable teamwork and problem-solving experience.

  • 2024 Camellia City RoboFest – Core Values Award 1st Place
  • 2024 State Championships – Innovation Project 2nd Place
